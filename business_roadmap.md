# Semantic Programming Platform
## Business Roadmap

### Elevator Pitch

We are building a **semantic programming platform** that understands software as a **graph of meaning instead of raw syntax**.

This enables:

• provably correct software  
• automatic algorithm optimization  
• safe AI-generated code  
• portable programs across hardware and operating systems  

The platform dramatically reduces **software bugs, infrastructure costs, and development time**.

Our initial market focuses on **cloud optimization and safety-critical software**, where a single bug or inefficiency can cost millions.
